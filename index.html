About us

Our philosophy is to offer simple and delicious desserts that quickly satisfy that craving for something sweet and homey. Our ingredients are of high quality; each dessert is carefully made to perfection to keep you coming back for more. Our treats are as deliciously wholesome as they are beautifully decorated. Choose from our signature cheesecakes, Florida Keylime pie, or latin favorites. With over a decade of restaurant and event experience, customer service is our top priority. We provide gracious and attentive service with every baked good which is what makes us great. We hope you'll allow us to show you first hand!
